1. B2B LOCAL BUYER LEADS ($5,250)

Targeting Travel Operators

Goal: Acquire 1,500+ registrations from local travel agents & outbound tour operators.

Targeting: Outbound travel agencies, tourism business owners, travel managers in Bangladesh.

Creative: Video ads detailing 'Hosted Buyer Benefits' (3 Nights Hotel, Pre-scheduled meetings, Networking dinners).

CPA: Target $3.50 per approved lead signup.

2. B2C GENERAL TICKETS ($3,750)

Targeting General Public

Goal: Sell 3,000+ visitor entry tickets online.

Targeting: Affluent families, honeymooners, and interest in international airlines and package tours in Dhaka.

Creative: Carousel ads highlighting exclusive discounts & packages from 130+ travel operators.

CPA: Target $1.20 per visitor ticket conversion.

LinkedIn Stall Sales ($3,750)

International Sellers Targeting

Goal: Capture 80+ high-quality corporate leads from foreign DMCs, hotel groups, and Tourism Boards (NTOs) to sell pavilions/stalls.

Geographic Focus: APAC & Middle East regions (Malaysia, Thailand, Turkey, Singapore, Saudi Arabia, Dubai).

CPA Target: $45.00 / Lead (Sales team to follow up directly to close stall contracts).

LinkedIn Targeting Details

Sectors: Leisure & Travel, Hospitality, Airlines, Government Relations.

Job Titles: GM, Director of Sales, Outbound Manager, Tourism Representative, Marketing Lead.

Job Functions: Business Development, Sales, Marketing, Executive.

Ad Formats: Document Ads distributing 'Outbound Tourism Growth in Bangladesh Market Report' (collects contact info to unlock).
