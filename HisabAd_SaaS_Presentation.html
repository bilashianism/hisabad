01 / CONCEPT SUMMARY

Executive Summary

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

An Independent "Referee" for Facebook Ads Spend

Thousands of Bangladeshi F-Commerce shop owners spend millions of BDT on Facebook Ads through marketing agencies. However, they lack the technical literacy to audit reports, resulting in blind trust and hidden wastage. HisabAd translates confusing Ads Manager spreadsheets into a simple, honest **Winning vs. Losing** grade.

0-100 ALGORITHM

The "Winning vs. Losing" Engine

  • 🟢 Winning (Score 70+): High ROAS, low CPA, high CTR. System suggests increasing ad budget.
  • 🟡 Tweak Needed (Score 40-69): Borderline ROAS but low CTR. Prompts user to swap ad images/creative.
  • 🔴 Losing (Score < 40): High spend, zero sales. Urges the merchant to pause campaigns immediately.
META COMPLIANCE

Dual-Onboarding Flows

  • Flow A (Self-Run Ads): Secure 1-click Facebook Login OAuth flow pulling direct ad insights from the Meta Graph API.
  • Flow B (Agency-Run Ads): Copyable requests wizard allowing merchants to send predefined, professional templates to their agency demanding View-Only Analyst rights.

1. Glassmorphism UI

* **Visual Theme:** Premium futuristic dark mode using radial gradients and translucent glass cards. * **Simplicity First:** Minimalist navigation containing only crucial numbers to prevent data fatigue.

2. Localization

* **Language Toggle:** Instant dashboard translation between English and Bangla. * **MFS Billing checkout:** A familiar, native-looking bKash, Nagad, and Rocket portal simulator built directly into the pricing journey.

3. WhatsApp Delivery

* **Zero-Login Reports:** Business owners receive structured PDF summaries sent straight to their WhatsApp or Messenger. * **Real-time Alerting:** Immediate notifications when a campaign drops below efficiency levels.

Boutique Agencies

* **Profile:** Small-to-medium digital marketing agencies managing ad campaigns for multiple clients. * **Pain Point:** Spending hours manually writing client reports. Wants white-labeled, simplified client dashboards.

* **Profile:** Small business founders running their own ads to drive sales but wasting money on unoptimized audiences. * **Pain Point:** High cost-per-purchase (CPA) eating their profit margins.
